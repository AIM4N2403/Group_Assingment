Company History

TechHaven isn't just a store; it's a dream realized. In 2017, a spark ignited in Che Muret Bin Ismail's heart - a passion for bringing top-notch electronics to everyone. This passion burned so brightly, it led Che Muret to take a leap of faith, leaving behind the familiar comfort of a day job. It was a bold move, fueled by a belief that exceptional electronics shouldn't be a privilege for a select few. From the humble beginnings of a home office, TechHaven has blossomed into a trusted name for tech enthusiasts across Kedah (our proud home!), Pulau Pinang, and Perlis. Every satisfied customer, every positive interaction, is a testament to the dedication and hard work that has gone into building this haven for all things electronic.

But TechHaven's story goes beyond products and sales. We understand the importance of responsible practices, and we're committed to being a force for good in the electronics industry. We believe that cutting-edge technology can go hand-in-hand with a greener tomorrow. Here at TechHaven, we aren't just selling electronics; we're offering a gateway to a world of possibilities.

We want you to be as excited about the tech you buy as we are about offering it. If you have any questions, if there's a way we can help you find the perfect gadget, or if you simply want to chat about the latest tech trends, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're here, fueled by the same passion that started it all, and ready to connect with you on your tech journey. Welcome to TechHaven, where quality meets passion, and customer service is our heart beat.
